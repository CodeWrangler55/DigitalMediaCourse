Course arc

From fundamentals to a public campaign system

Students begin with visual communication, typography, color, and critique. They then move into digital imaging, vector systems, layout, web visuals, and short promotional video before assembling a final brand campaign case study.

  1. Weeks 1-4Design foundations, typography, color, research
  2. Weeks 5-8Image editing, vector work, identity systems
  3. Weeks 9-12Layout, digital publishing, web and accessibility
  4. Weeks 13-16Short video, capstone production, presentation

Capstone

Final project: brand campaign system

Students create a complete campaign for a fictional or local organization, including logo, image treatment, print collateral, social graphics, landing page visual direction, and one 15-30 second video or motion social post.
