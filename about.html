Instructor framing

Teaching Digital Media Through Useful, Finished Work

Chris White builds this course around a direct premise: students learn design best when each skill becomes a concrete piece they can critique, revise, explain, and add to a portfolio.

Teaching Approach

My teaching approach combines demonstration, guided practice, critique, and revision. Students are expected to learn vocabulary and technique, but the deeper goal is judgment: knowing why a design choice helps or hurts the message.

The course is structured for students with uneven preparation. Each project includes a clear brief, examples, process checkpoints, and a rubric that rewards both visual quality and professional habits.
