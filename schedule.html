16-week plan

Semester Schedule

The semester moves from design fundamentals to a complete brand campaign system, including a dedicated two-week short video unit.

Week 1

Orientation and Visual Communication

Course expectations, design vocabulary, audience, message, and critique norms.

Studio outcome: Visual hierarchy audit.
Week 2

Design Principles and Composition

Contrast, alignment, proximity, repetition, balance, grid, focal point, and revision.

Studio outcome: Poster or flyer redesign.
Week 3

Typography Foundations

Type anatomy, pairing, scale, spacing, readability, and expressive type choices.

Studio outcome: Typographic poster or type specimen.
Week 4

Color, Brand Mood, and Visual Research

Mood boards, color systems, reference gathering, and design direction.

Studio outcome: Campaign mood board and palette sheet.
Week 5

Digital Imaging Fundamentals

Resolution, file formats, selections, masks, correction, cleanup, and export.

Studio outcome: Image correction and retouching study.
Week 6

Advanced Composite Design

Layered imagery, lighting consistency, texture, typography integration, and ethical image use.

Studio outcome: Composite campaign image.
Week 7

Vector Illustration and Logo Construction

Shape building, curves, icon systems, logo marks, simplification, and scalability.

Studio outcome: Logo mark and icon set.
Week 8

Brand Identity System

Logo usage, color, type, spacing, voice, consistency, and small brand guides.

Studio outcome: Mini brand guide.
Week 9

Page Design and Layout

Print grids, hierarchy, body copy, captions, margins, columns, and production files.

Studio outcome: Brochure, flyer, or editorial spread.
Week 10

Digital Publishing and Campaign Collateral

Social graphics, email graphics, infographics, multi-size assets, and export workflows.

Studio outcome: Social campaign set.
Week 11

Web and Interactive Visual Design

Landing page structure, responsive layout, visual hierarchy online, and asset preparation.

Studio outcome: Campaign landing page design.
Week 12

UI, Usability, and Accessible Design

Navigation, contrast, readability, alt text, user tasks, and design improvement.

Studio outcome: Accessible interface mockup.
Week 13

Video for Designers I

Story, shot planning, storyboards, frame composition, capture, and rough cuts.

Studio outcome: 15-30 second storyboard and rough cut.
Week 14

Video for Designers II

Editing, pacing, titles, sound, captions, compression, and export for social or web.

Studio outcome: Finished short campaign video.
Week 15

Capstone Production and Critique

Campaign assembly, peer critique, production polish, and case study drafting.

Studio outcome: Complete campaign draft.
Week 16

Final Portfolio Presentation

Final case study, oral presentation, reflection, and portfolio readiness.

Studio outcome: Final campaign case study.
